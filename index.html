VISION

Vision of Government Polytechnic College,Purapuzha of Engineering Diploma is to mould Engineers for tomorrow for the welfare of mankind and Society. We aim to give quality education for students with values added thoughts. Our academic motto includes quality education, Ethical Values and Spiritual deeds and conducts of all in our Family.

MISSION

We commit to develop the institution as a Center of Excellence of International Standards We guide our students in the attainment of intellectual and professional competence for successfully coping with the rapid advancements in technologies and the ever changing world of business, industry and services We help each and every student in their personal growth into mature and responsible individuals We strive to cultivate a sense of social and civic responsibility in our students, thus empowering them to serve the humanity We promise to ensure a free environment where quest for the truth is encouraged

Government Polytechnic College, Purapuzha is one of the major Technical institutions in Idukki district established by the Government of Kerala in the year 2000 situated at Purapuzha, 8 Kms from Thodupuzha. This institution offers three year diploma Programmes in two disciplines of Engineering / Technology (Computer Engineering (CT) and Information Technology (IF)) under the Directorate of Technical Education, Govt. of Kerala. The institution is approved by the AICTE.Government Polytechnic College, Purapuzha is one of the major Technical institution in Idukki district situated at Purapuzha, Thodupuzha. It functions along with other educational institutions in Central Travancore, which carries a creditable record of supplying several technicians and a group of talented persons to the nation. Government Polytechnic College, Purapuzha was established in the year 2000 which came an upgradation of a Technical High School, which had been functioning here since 2000. Located in a rural area on a hillock , the scenic beauty and serenity of the surroundings appears most suited for academic pursuits. The institution stands less than one kilometers from Purapuzha junction, which is only 9 km away from Thodupuzha. Regular bus services on this major route between Thodupuzha and Vazhithala make journey to the College quite an easy thing. At present the institution is in a campus of 15 acres at Purapuzha,Thodupuzha. This institution offers three year diploma Programmes in two major disciplines of Engineering / Technology ( Computer Engg. and Information Technology ) under the Directorateof Technical Education, Govt. of Kerala. The institution is approved by the ALL INDIA COUNCIL FOR TECHNICAL EDUCATION, NEW DELHI

ABOUT PLACEMENT CELL

The Department of Computer Engineering has become a full-fledged department with advanced laboratory facilities to train the students to meet the current needs of the fast changing industrial scenario.The Computer Engineering Department provides excellent environment for training in computer hardware and software. With ever increasing demand for software and hardware professionals in IT field , the course provides ample opportunities for employment in India and abroad. The department offers three year Diploma course in Computer Engineering. The annual intake is 67 students. The curriculum has been systematically designed to mould a competent Computer engineer to cater the needs of industry and R&D. The Department of Information Technology has become a full-fledged department with advanced laboratory facilities to train the students to meet the current needs of the fast changing industrial scenario.The Information Technology Department provides excellent environment for training in computer hardware and software. With ever increasing demand for software and hardware professionals in IT field , the course provides ample opportunities for employment in India and abroad. The department offers three year Diploma course in Information Technology. The annual intake is 66 students. The curriculum has been systematically designed to mould a competent IT to cater the needs of industry and R&D. Periodical updation of the syllabus is also done in order to keep with changes in modern software and hardware fields.

Various co-curricular and extra-curricular activities are organised for the development of skills in students.
